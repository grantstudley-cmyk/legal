Privacy Policy

Application: Midnight Horror Tales
Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Midnight Horror Tales application (the "App") handles information. The App is a personal content-automation tool operated by its owner ("we", "us", "our") to create and publish original short videos to social media accounts that we own and control. We have built the App to access the minimum data necessary and to keep that data on the operator's own device.

1. Information We Access

DataWhyWhere it is stored
TikTok OAuth access & refresh tokens To authorize the App to upload videos to the connected TikTok account via the official Content Posting API. Locally, on the operator's own computer only.
Basic TikTok account identifier / display name To confirm which account the App is authorized to post to. Locally, on the operator's own computer only.
Video files and captions created by the operator To publish or schedule them to the operator's own account. Locally, on the operator's own computer only.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect data from any third party's account. It does not gather your followers' personal information, browsing activity, location, contacts, or advertising identifiers. It does not run analytics or tracking on end users.

3. How We Use Information

Access tokens and account identifiers are used exclusively to authenticate with the TikTok API and to upload or schedule the operator's own video content. We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Information is transmitted only to the platform you connect to (TikTok) for the sole purpose of publishing your content, in accordance with that platform's API.

5. Third-Party Services

  • TikTok Content Posting API — receives your video and caption in order to publish it to your account. See the TikTok Privacy Policy.
  • Wikimedia Commons — queried for openly licensed background imagery. No personal data is sent.
  • Text-to-speech generation — narration audio is generated from text provided by the operator; no personal user data is transmitted.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Tokens and content remain on the operator's device until deleted. You can revoke the App's access at any time from your TikTok account settings ("Security & permissions" → "Manage app permissions"), and you can delete the locally stored token file at any time. Once access is revoked, the App can no longer act on your account.

7. Security

Access tokens are stored locally and are not published or transmitted to any server other than the official TikTok API endpoints over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended only for use by its adult operator and is not directed to children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest revision.

10. Contact
